The Mission

Simplifying Personal Finance

BudgetCalc was born out of a simple idea: that financial planning tools should be accessible, private, and easy to use. We believe that taking control of your money shouldn't require a complex spreadsheet or a paid subscription.

Our tool is designed to provide instant clarity on your monthly spending, helping you visualize the 50/30/20 rule and find room for savings without the friction of creating an account.
