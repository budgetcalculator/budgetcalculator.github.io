Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

At BudgetCalc, we prioritize your financial privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our free online budget calculator.

1. No Personal Data Collection

BudgetCalc is designed to be a "privacy-first" tool. We do not require sign-ups, accounts, or email addresses to use our planner. Any financial data you enter—such as income, housing costs, or savings goals—is processed locally in your browser.

2. How Data is Stored

We do not store your financial data on our servers. Your budget information is saved in your browser's Local Storage. This allows you to close the page and return later with your data intact. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache or using the "Reset" function within the tool.

3. Third-Party Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-focused analytics (like Plausible or basic Google Analytics) to understand site traffic. These tools do not see or record the financial values you enter into the calculator.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to remember your settings (like currency preferences). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository or support email.
