POLICY

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to My Budget ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we treat the information you enter into our financial calculators.

2. Data Collection

Calculated Data: Any financial information you enter into our calculators (income, debts, tax details, or home prices) is processed locally on your device or via secure temporary sessions. We do NOT store your personal financial numbers in a database.

Cookies & Tracking: We use Google Analytics to monitor site traffic. This helps us understand which calculators are most useful to our visitors. This data is anonymous and does not identify you personally.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics. These third parties may collect information such as your IP address and browser type to provide us with website performance metrics.

4. Security

Because our calculators are "client-side" focused, your sensitive financial data remains in your browser. However, we encourage users to always use a secure connection when performing financial planning.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date and will be effective as soon as it is accessible.
