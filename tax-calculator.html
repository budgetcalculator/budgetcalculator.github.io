Take Control of Your Paycheck

Ever look at your paystub and wonder where half the money went? You aren't alone. Between federal withholding, state taxes, and FICA, it feels like everyone gets a piece of your pie before you do. That’s why we built this advanced tax calculator.

Whether you’re planning for a raise or just trying to figure out your monthly budget planner needs, knowing your true net income is the first step toward financial freedom.

How to Use This Tax Estimator

Our tool is designed to be a free online tax planner that gives you instant results. Follow these steps:

  • 1. Enter Gross Income: This is your total salary before any taxes are taken out.
  • 2. Select Status: Your filing status (Single, Married, etc.) drastically changes your tax brackets.
  • 3. Account for Deductions: If you use the standard deduction, enter it here to see your taxable income.

Understanding FICA and Federal Tax

Most people forget about FICA. That’s the 7.65% that goes toward Social Security and Medicare. Our income tax calculator factors this in automatically so your "Take Home" amount is as accurate as possible.

If you're using our budget calculator with percentages, use the "Est. Take Home" value from this page as your starting "Income" for the most accurate 50/30/20 results.

Why Estimating Taxes Matters

Surprises are great for birthdays, not for tax season. By using an online income tax estimator, you can adjust your withholdings or save more throughout the year. This pairs perfectly with our paycheck budget calculator to ensure you are never living beyond your means.
