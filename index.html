FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the 50/30/20 rule and how does it work? +

The 50/30/20 rule is a simple budgeting method where you allocate 50% of your after-tax income to needs (housing, food, utilities), 30% to wants (entertainment, dining out), and 20% to savings and debt repayment. Our calculator automatically computes these amounts based on your income.

Is my financial data stored or saved? +

No! All calculations happen in your browser. We don't store any of your financial information on our servers. Your privacy is 100% protected.

Can I use this for business budgeting? +

While designed for personal finance, many small business owners find our calculator useful for basic business budgeting. For complex business needs, consider specialized business accounting software.

How often should I update my budget? +

We recommend reviewing and updating your budget at least monthly. However, if you experience significant income changes or unexpected expenses, adjust immediately.

What's the difference between basic and advanced calculator? +

The basic calculator gives you a quick surplus/deficit number. The advanced calculator breaks down expenses into categories and provides detailed insights about your spending patterns and savings rate.
